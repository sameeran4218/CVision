Job Title: Data Scientist
Location: Bangalore, India (Hybrid)
Company: AlphaTech Analytics Pvt. Ltd.
Experience: 1–3 years
Salary: ₹10–18 LPA

About the Role
We are looking for a passionate Data Scientist to join our growing team. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to uncover insights from large volumes of data and build data-driven solutions to support business decisions.

Key Responsibilities
Analyze large datasets to identify patterns, trends, and actionable insights.

Develop predictive and classification models using machine learning algorithms.

Collaborate with product and engineering teams to deploy data solutions into production.

Communicate findings through effective visualizations and presentations.

Perform A/B testing and statistical analysis for product experimentation.

Required Skills
Strong proficiency in Python for data analysis and machine learning.

Hands-on experience with pandas, NumPy, scikit-learn, and Matplotlib/Seaborn.

Solid understanding of statistics, probability, and machine learning concepts.

Experience working with SQL and relational databases.

Familiarity with version control (Git) and Jupyter notebooks.

Preferred Qualifications
Experience with big data tools (e.g., Spark, Hadoop) is a plus.

Exposure to cloud platforms like AWS, GCP, or Azure.

Knowledge of NLP or computer vision is a bonus.

Education
Bachelor's or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Mathematics, Statistics, or a related field.
